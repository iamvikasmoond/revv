Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ride?

You can book directly from the home screen. Enter your destination, choose your vehicle, and confirm your booking.

What are the rental charges?

Charges depend on vehicle type and duration. You’ll always see the estimated fare before confirming your booking.

How do I cancel my booking?

Go to “Your Rides,” select the booking, and tap “Cancel Ride.” Cancellation fees may apply based on timing.
